Business Risks & Pain Points Tied to Planview
Execution Credibility Risk

With 5+ simultaneous transformation bets and aggressive 2026 revenue targets, Visa's leadership faces intense board scrutiny on execution. CA Clarity's inability to provide real-time portfolio status, dynamic reprioritization, and cross-group visibility creates credibility risk at every QBR.

Multi-Group Alignment Gaps

Value Services, GPC, and Tech each use different planning processes. Without a unified platform, resource contention, shadow IT, and subjective prioritization ("loudest voice wins") erode confidence in the portfolio. This is particularly acute as Visa scales its AI commerce investments.

Regulatory & Cybersecurity Pressure

Visa's 10-K (FY2025) cites cybersecurity and technology investment governance as key enterprise risks. The board-level audit and risk committee has direct oversight of technology program risk. Poor portfolio visibility amplifies the board's exposure to unresolved program risks.
